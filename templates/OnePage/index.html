Bible Shake

Bible Shake generates random scriptures and provides a unique way to navigate the Bible. Shake your device to display a random verse or chapter, or use the spinner wheels to quickly jump to specific verses. Bible shake currently offers full KJV and ASV Bible versions and provides a range of filter options so you can decide where the random verse will be selected from. Use Bible Shake to randomly select a daily bible reading or devotion, or use it as your main Bible App. Android App

SmartMed

SmartMed Telehealth is the remote monitoring of patient vital signs, symptoms and general wellbeing using the latest mobile and Internet technologies. SmartMed allows patients to take their own readings from the comfort of their own home and the information is then remotely assessed by qualified healthcare professionals. The healthcare provider can then intervene in a timely and efficient manner, ensuring the patient gets the best possible Individualised care..
